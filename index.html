Hello!

My name is Teddy Walsh (in case you thought I lied in the header...) and I am a game design and development student enrolled at Rochester Institute of Technology. I've been making games for years and years at this point.

It started back in middle school when I would make games in Minecraft with my best friend programmed entirely using in-game command blocks, without mods, for all our friends to play. Ever since then, I've developed a hunger for game design.

Before I knew how to program myself, that hunger had been expressed in the form of designing table top RPGs and D&D 5e homebrew consistently for nearly 5 years now. After learning engines like Unity and languages like C#, I'm starting to transfer this passion into something more palpable.

I would love to work on your project! To do that though, I imagine I'll need to show some of my projects. That's fair... That's fair...
