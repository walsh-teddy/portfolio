Skill Drain

5th-level enchantment

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: S, V

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour


You reach into the mind of one creature and attempt to temporarily steal training from their memory. An unwilling creature must make an intelligence saving throw to avoid the effect.

You choose one proficiency with a weapon, piece of armor, tool, language, or skill; or a prepared spell that is also in your class's spell list. For the duration you gain any bonuses or ability to speak/understand or ability to cast your chosen training, while the target loses it. If you choose a training that the creature benefits from a feature that increases their proficiency with it, such as expertise, you also benefit from that feature.
