By Teddy Walsh

Arcane Analysis

6th-level divination

Classes: Bard, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 ft

Components: V, S, M (A magnifying glass or periscope)

Duration: Instantaneous


You attempt to learn the traits and abilities of a creature by scanning them with magic. They must make a deception check vs your spell save DC. If they fail, you automatically learn all their ability scores, their maximum and current health, their creature type, and 3 traits of your choice from the following list.

  • Their armor class
  • Their speed(s)
  • Their saving throw proficiencies
  • Their skill proficiencies
  • Their senses
  • Their languages
  • Their spell list as well as current and maximum spell slots
  • 1 random trait (not including spell casting if you chose the option before this one)
  • 1 random action or reaction

The options can be chosen one at a time after hearing the ones before, and the final 2 can be chosen any number of times, not repeating random traits and actions you already learned about using this spell.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can learn an additional 2 traits for each slot level above 6.
