Breathless

2nd-level necromancy

Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration up to 1 minute


You reach out with magic and grab a creature’s breath before they can pull it back into their body. They must make a constitution saving throw or be unable to breath air, water, or any other substance and start suffocating. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of their turn, ending the spell on a success. Creatures that don't need to breathe such as most undead or constructs are immune to this spell.

At Higher Levels. When casting this spell using a spell slot of 2rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each level above 2nd.

Note: The rules for suffocating are on page 183 of the Player’s Handbook
