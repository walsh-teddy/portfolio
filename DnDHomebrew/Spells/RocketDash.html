Rocket Dash

3rd-level evocation

Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S, M (a small bottle of flammable liquid)

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes


Jets of fire burst out of your hands and feet allowing you to fly. A 10 ft cone of fire bursts out from you in a direction you choose and you are pushed 20 ft in the opposite direction and gain 40 ft of flying speed until the end of your next turn. All creatures in that cone must succeed on a dexterity saving throw or take 2d6 fire damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one. You can use your bonus action to repeat this effect on each of your turns for the duration.

At Higher Levels. When this spell is casted using a 4th level spell slot or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each level of spell slot used above 3rd.
