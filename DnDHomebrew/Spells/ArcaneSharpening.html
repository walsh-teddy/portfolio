Arcane Sharpening

1st-level transmutation (ritual)

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Paladin, Ranger, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (1 gp worth of crushed gems, which the spell consumes)

Duration: 8 hours


Touch one weapon or group of 10 ammunition. They count as magical and silvered for the purposes of overcoming resistances and immunities for the duration.
