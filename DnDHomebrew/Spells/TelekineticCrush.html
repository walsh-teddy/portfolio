Telekinetic Crush

3rd-level transmutation (dunemancy)

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: S, M

Duration: Instantaneous


You summon physical force to push in on target a creature from all sides attempting to crush them. They must succeed on a strength saving throw or take 5d10 bludgeoning damage, taking half on a success. If this damage reduces a creature to 0 health, their body is crushed into a ball.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 3rd.
