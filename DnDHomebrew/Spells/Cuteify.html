By Teddy Walsh

Name

2nd-level transmutation

Classes: Bard, Druid, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 ft

Components: V, S, M (A glob of pink cotton and a pair of googly eyes)

Duration: 1 minute


You attempt to transmute a horrifying creature into a goofy and/or cute one, still just as deadly though. The creature must make a wisdom saving throw or be transmuted for the duration. While transmuted, it is unable to be the source of fear and has disadvantage on intimidation checks. It can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
