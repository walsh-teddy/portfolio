Magical Insulation

8th-level abjuration

Classes: Bard, Cleric, Warlock, Wizard

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (A diamond worth at least 5,000 gp, which the spell consumes)

Duration: 24 hours


You attempt to completely disconnect a creature within range from any force of magic. They must make a charisma saving throw (they can willingly fail if they choose) or be insulated by magic for the duration, causing the following effects for the duration:

  • They are unable to cast spells
  • They are unable to invoke magical effects or abilities
  • They are unable to use magic items
  • They have advantage on all saving throws against spells or other magical effects
  • They have resistance to all damage caused by spells or other magical effects
  • They have immunity to force damage

This also ends any spell that was affecting them casted at a lower level than the spell slot level used to cast this. If this creature is held together or kept alive through magic, such as an iron golem or a litch, they must make a constitution saving throw or take 10d10 force damage at the start of each of their turns, taking half on a success.

Ability checks made to remove this effect, such as through the dispel magic spell, have disadvantage.
